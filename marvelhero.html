SPIDER MAN

Spider man informations...

Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is one of the most iconic and beloved superheroes in the Marvel Universe. Created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, Spider-Man made his first appearance in "Amazing Fantasy" #15 in August 1962. Here are some key details about Spider-Man:

Origin:
Spider-Man's origin story revolves around a young and brilliant high school student named Peter Parker. He was bitten by a radioactive spider during a science experiment, which granted him spider-like abilities.
Powers and Abilities:
Spider-Man possesses a range of superhuman powers, including superhuman strength, agility, and reflexes.
He has the ability to cling to walls and surfaces, much like a spider.
Spider-Man's "Spider-Sense" is an extrasensory perception that alerts him to danger.
He's a skilled hand-to-hand combatant and has created his own web-shooters to swing between buildings and create various web-based tools and weapons.

MOVIES

Sam Raimi's Spider-Man Trilogy

"Spider-Man" (2002) - Directed by Sam Raimi, this film starred Tobey Maguire as Peter Parker/Spider-Man and introduced the character to a new generation. It focuses on Spider-Man's origins and his battle with the Green Goblin.
"Spider-Man 2" (2004) - Often regarded as one of the best superhero films, this sequel sees Spider-Man facing off against Doctor Octopus.
"Spider-Man 3" (2007) - This installment sees Peter Parker dealing with the symbiote, which leads to the creation of the villain Venom.



The Amazing Spider-Man Series:

"The Amazing Spider-Man" (2012) - Directed by Marc Webb, this reboot starred Andrew Garfield as Spider-Man. It explores Peter Parker's high school years and his initial encounters with villains like the Lizard.
"The Amazing Spider-Man 2" (2014) - The sequel introduces Electro, Green Goblin, and the Rhino as villains.

Marvel's Spider-Man: Miles Morales (2020):
This game, also developed by Insomniac Games and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment, is a standalone expansion to "Marvel's Spider-Man." Players take on the role of Miles Morales, who has his own set of powers and abilities. The game continues to explore the story of the Spider-Verse.
